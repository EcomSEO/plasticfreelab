# PlasticFreeLab — Affiliate Partner Reference

The affiliate brands we'll review across Wave 1 comparison posts, the networks that carry them, and the application strategy for a brand-new site.

**Last updated:** April 2026. Commission rates and network coverage drift — verify every entry before onboarding.

**Approval reality check:** A brand-new domain with zero traffic is not approved to Amazon Associates, ShareASale merchants, or Impact programs on day one. See §9 for the order of operations. Do not apply to any affiliate program until:
1. Home page + at least 10 live posts + all trust pages are live and indexed.
2. Privacy policy, terms, and FTC affiliate disclosure pages are live (all three exist in our repo already).
3. A branded email on the apex domain (`hello@plasticfreelab.com`) is reachable.
4. Google Search Console is verified and returning traffic (even single digits is fine).

Jumping the gun usually means a rejection that takes 30-60 days to re-apply.

## 1. Affiliate networks we'll use

| Network | Covers | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Amazon Associates** | Most physical-product brands that also sell on Amazon | Low commissions (1–4.5% depending on category), but near-universal coverage. Apply after site has content. |
| **ShareASale (Awin subsidiary)** | Mid-market DTC brands — Caraway, Parachute, Branch Basics, many organic brands | Higher commissions (8–15% common). Merchant-by-merchant approval. |
| **Impact.com** | Premium DTC + sustainability-forward brands — Our Place, Brandless, Grove Collaborative, Eight Sleep | Growing in this category. Per-merchant approval. |
| **Awin** | European brands + some US crossover | Worth joining after US brands are set up. |
| **Rakuten Advertising (LinkShare)** | Williams-Sonoma, Macy's, some Target-owned brands | Useful for "big retailer" alternatives. |
| **AvantLink** | Outdoor / sustainability-forward — Avocado Mattress, some smaller organic brands | Niche but strong in our vertical. |
| **Pepperjam (Partnerize)** | Beauty + personal care — some Credo-network brands | Verify current merchant list. |
| **Direct affiliate programs** | Brands with in-house programs — Berkey, some mattress brands | Usually highest commissions (12–20%) but more admin. |

**Application order:** Amazon Associates first (easiest approval once you have 10+ posts), ShareASale second (apply to network, then to 5–10 merchants at once), Impact third, direct programs as you grow.

## 9. Application order of operations (first 90 days)

**Month 1:** Build trust. Zero affiliate links live. Just the site, the trust pages, the first 10 posts.

**Month 2 — after first 10 posts are live:**
1. **Amazon Associates** — apply. Approval usually 1-7 days if site has content. Link to ~50 products across the first 10 posts. Make first qualifying sale within 180 days to stay approved.
2. **ShareASale** — apply to the network. Approval usually 1-3 days. Then apply to the top 10 merchants listed above (Made In, Caraway, Avocado, Pact, Branch Basics, etc.). Each merchant approves individually, 1-14 days.
3. **Impact.com** — apply to network, then apply to Our Place, Eight Sleep, Grove Collaborative, etc.

**Month 3 — after 20+ posts and initial traffic:**
4. **Direct affiliate programs:** AquaTru, Berkey, Clearly Filtered, Boka, RiseWell, Avocado (if not via AvantLink), Smithey, Stargazer. These pay highest commissions but require manual email outreach in some cases.
5. **AvantLink** — apply. Needed for Avocado and a few niche outdoor/sustainability brands.
6. **Rakuten Advertising** — apply if we want Williams-Sonoma / Target.

**Month 4+:** Evaluate performance per merchant, prune underperformers, double down on the top 5 revenue drivers.

## 11. Rejection recovery

Affiliate programs reject new sites. Common reasons and fixes:

| Reason | Fix |
|---|---|
| "Insufficient content" | Wait until 15+ posts published; re-apply |
| "Missing privacy policy" | We already have one at `/privacy` — link it in the application |
| "No traffic data" | Skip the merchant until Google Search Console shows ≥100 weekly impressions |
| "Too promotional" | Make sure the site isn't just "buy these things" — informational content ratio should be high |
| "Branding unclear" | Make sure `/about` is clear and professional |

Many merchants auto-reject applications from free email domains (Gmail, Outlook). Always apply using `hello@plasticfreelab.com` or similar on the custom domain.

## 12. Revenue model — rough Wave 1 math

Assumptions:
- 10,000 monthly sessions by month 6
- 2% click-through to affiliate link (Wirecutter benchmark is ~3-5%)
- 3% conversion rate on clicks (industry-standard for comparison content)
- $35 blended commission per conversion (weighted average across categories, dominated by mattress + water filter)

**10,000 × 0.02 × 0.03 × $35 = ~$210/month at month 6.**

That's the floor. Mattress traffic alone can multiply this 3-5× when a single high-intent reader converts. Realistic target by month 12 at 50k sessions: **$2,500–5,000/month**.

Compound-slowly math. Not VC scale. But per the CLAUDE.md editorial promise — compound the trust first, the revenue follows.
